Preparing for your session

  • Find a stable wifi connection — a strong signal keeps the session smooth and lets you share your screen without interruption.
  • Choose a quiet, private space where you feel comfortable speaking freely. Background noise and interruptions can break your flow.
  • Have your laptop or desktop ready. You'll get more out of the session if you can try things in real time alongside your coach.
  • If you're working toward a specific project, have any relevant files, tools, or accounts open and ready to go.
  • Jot down one or two things you most want to walk away with. Even a rough intention helps focus the session.
  • This session is entirely personalized for you — feel free to ask us to slow down, go deeper, or skip ahead at any point. Your pace is the right pace.
  • There are no wrong questions. If something isn't clicking, say so — that's exactly the kind of moment coaching is built for.
  • You don't need to prepare anything formal. Show up curious. That's enough.
